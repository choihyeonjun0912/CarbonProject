# Constants

## tokens.ts
Material 3 forest palette: `primary` (#00261a), `primaryContainer` (#0f3d2e), `secondary` (#126c40), surface tones, fixed accent greens. Typography: hero (32/800), h1 (24/700), label (11/700 uppercase). Includes `shadows.botanical` and `shadows.button`.

## species.ts
`SPECIES` array: pine (6.6 kg/yr), oak (14.0), acacia (8.8), other (8.0 default). `getSpecies(id)` lookup returns other if not found. CO2 rates from Korea Forest Research Institute data.
